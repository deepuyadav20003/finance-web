About Geoffrey Hemenway

Geoffrey J. Hemenway is a Financial Advisor and Financial Planner, in Kaneohe. He joined Hemenway Financial Services, a group of independent advisors registered with LPL Financial, in 2014. In 2020, Geoff purchased Hemenway Financial Services from his late grandfather, Joseph Hemenway.

Geoff is passionate about helping individuals and families to plan and work toward their unique goals as they approach and enjoy retirement (or as he likes to call it - Financial Independence).

Geoff proudly has a Bachelor of Science degree from Creighton University. He enjoys spending time with his wife, Shannon, and son, Joseph, along with friends and family. He is also a big sports fan and enjoys traveling

About Hemenway Financial Services

Here at Hemenway Financial Services, we hope to help you and your family find a path towards achieving your goals. We do this by working with you to build a comprehensive financial plan or, if needed, individual topic planning. In doing this, we hope to align what's important to you (family, travel, financial independence, charity/community etc.) with how you utilize and how you invest your money.

We work mostly with people that are approaching (5-10 years before) and in retirement. We feel that we have a strong history of helping families to plan for this stage in life. We've also helped several of our client's adult children with planning in the different stages of their life.

We'd love an opportunity to see if we are a good fit for you and your situation.

FAQs

Why should I choose you as my financial advisor?

I think each person/family should choose a financial advisor that they feel comfortable with and they believe can help them to pursue whatever goals they are trying to accomplish.

How do you charge for your services?

I'm a fee-based advisor, that charges a percentage based on assets under management. This is tiered or blended rate and starts at 1% under $1 million and drops 0.1% for each additional million. For example a $2,000,000 household would be 1% for the first million and 0.9% for the second million averaging out to 0.95% for the total. Above $5,000,000 the fee is a flat 0.5% and is not tiered as previously discussed. If there is a unique or particular need that a specific product is necessary, there may be commissions that our office receives.

What services do you provide? What are your specialties?

We provide comprehensive financial planning or targeted planning, depending upon need and urgency. I work with individuals and families between $500,000-$5,000,000 in investable assets that are approaching or in retirement.

What is your experience? How many clients do you currently manage?

I have been in the industry for 9 years and am also an owner within a family owned and operated tax and accounting office. I currently have approximately 75 Advisory Clients that are fee-based. Overall, our office manages a total that is approximately 250 households.

What financial institution(s) do you represent?

We are independent advisors registered with LPL Financial.
