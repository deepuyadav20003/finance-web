Who We Are

WiserAdvisor.com is an independent matching service that helps individuals find the best financial advisor for their unique needs. We have successfully helped over 100,000+ individuals like you find their ideal financial advisor since 1998.

In order to join our network, each and every advisor must pass our three-step qualification process. We screen advisors based on their years of experience, their SEC/FINRA registration and records, and their compensation criterion. All advisors on our network are fee only or fee based.

What We Do

We make it easy and convenient for consumers to find and connect with pre-screened financial advisors in their area.
