About Pleso Capital Group

We are a fee-only, independent firm committed to helping our clients achieve their financial goals through a personalized approach.

We take the guesswork out of identifying, planning for, and reaching your goals. Schedule a free call with us to get a feel of how we can work together.

If you decide to continue, our process starts with understanding your situation. This second, longer call is designed for us to find your purpose, by digging deeper with what was discussed in the first meeting. Depending on complexity, we will meet several times over the next few weeks as we continue to understand your unique situation.

After we have reviewed your details including income, investments, insurance, and taxes, we will make our recomendations to you. Once you are satisfied, we can help put our plan in action. Once the plan is place, we will continue to update and adjust as necessary. We will meet periodically throughout the year to check in, but you are always free to contact us if you have concerns anytime.
