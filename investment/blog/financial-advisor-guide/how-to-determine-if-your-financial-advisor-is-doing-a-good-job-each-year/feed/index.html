Armey https://www.wiseradvisor.com/blog/financial-advisor-guide/how-to-determine-if-your-financial-advisor-is-doing-a-good-job-each-year/#comment-7952 Tue, 10 Sep 2024 15:25:59 +0000 https://www.wiseradvisor.com/blog/?p=4672#comment-7952 Instead of asking financial advisors, ‘Am I beating what is normal?’ a client should ask, ‘Did I achieve a good risk-adjusted return for my personal situation and goals?’ This is a more insightful approach.

To effectively evaluate your financial advisor, always ask questions like:

– If I lose 10% of my investment in year one and make 10% back in year two, how much have I gained or lost?
– Can I pay you hourly, and how does that work?
– If interest rates go up, does the value of a bond fund increase or decrease?
– Do you have your own money in what you recommend to me? Can you prove that?
